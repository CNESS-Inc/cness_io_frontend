1. PURPOSE OF OUR COMMUNITY

CNESS exists to unite conscious individuals, professionals, organizations, and partners into a thriving ecosystem of collaboration, knowledge sharing, ethical commerce, and personal and collective growth.

Our communities serve as spaces for:

  • Conscious conversations
  • Thoughtful knowledge exchange
  • Networking and collaboration
  • Leadership development
  • Marketplace and project initiatives aligned with conscious values

2. EXPECTATIONS OF BEHAVIOR

We expect all members of the CNESS community to uphold the following principles:

  1. a. Respect and Courtesy:
  • Treat every individual with dignity and respect, regardless of background, profession, beliefs, or views.
  • Engage in dialogue with kindness, empathy, and active listening.
  1. b. Relevance and Constructiveness:
  • Contribute insights, questions, or content that are purposeful and relevant to the CNESS’s mission.
  • Offer constructive feedback; avoid personal attacks, hostility, or inflammatory language.
  1. c. Inclusivity and Openness:
  • Foster an inclusive environment free from discrimination, harassment, or exclusion.
  • Support diversity of thought and experiences.
  1. d. Responsibility and Integrity:
  • Share accurate information to the best of your knowledge.
  • Respect confidentiality, intellectual property rights, and privacy expectations.
  • Represent CNESS and your affiliations honestly and ethically.

    • a physically harmful substance;
    • a substance in such a quantity as to be physically harmful.

Advertising

Hidden advertising (where a content creator has been paid or otherwise has an incentive to endorse a product but does not clearly identify the content as advertising) is not allowed on our site.

You must not upload a video containing advertising for any of the following:

  • Cigarettes and other tobacco products, electronic cigarettes or electronic cigarette refill containers, and prescription-only medicine; or
  • Alcoholic drinks that are not aimed specifically at under 18s and do not encourage immoderate consumption of alcohol.

Reviews

Fake reviews are not allowed on our service. Fake reviews include reviews that conceal that the reviewer has been incentivized in some way to post the review, or where the review contains information that is false or misleading.

4. PROHIBITED CONDUCT

The following activities are strictly prohibited on the Services and in all community interactions:

  1. a. Harassment, Bullying, or Hate Speech:
  • Any form of harassment, threats, abuse, or intimidation.
  • Hate speech based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or any protected characteristic.
  1. b. Unlawful Content or Conduct:
  • Promotion or engagement in illegal activities.
  • Sharing of illicit, obscene, sexually explicit, or violent materials.
  1. c. Spam, Scams, and Self-Promotion:
  • Posting repetitive, unsolicited promotions or irrelevant commercial content.
  • Fraudulent schemes, phishing attempts, or misleading solicitations.
  1. d. Privacy Violations:
  • Sharing others' private, confidential, or personally identifiable information without explicit consent.
  • Doxxing, recording, or distributing conversations without permission.
  1. e. Intellectual Property Infringement:
  • Sharing content that you do not own or lack permission to distribute.
  • Misusing copyrighted materials, trademarks, or proprietary information.
