CNESS COMMUNITY GUIDELINES & CODE OF CONDUCT

Effective Date: [To be updated upon publishing]

Welcome to the CNESS community. At Cness Inc. ("CNESS," "we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to fostering a conscious, collaborative, and respectful environment across our Platform, including all websites and services operated through www.cness.io, www.cness.co, www.cness.ai, www.cnesscertified.com, and www.cnesslive.com ("Platform").

These Community Guidelines and Code of Conduct ("Guidelines") apply to all Users participating in any community feature, event, discussion forum, group, directory, mentorship, marketplace interaction, certification cohort, or social media activity associated with CNESS.

By engaging with the CNESS community, you agree to comply with these Guidelines.

1. PURPOSE OF OUR COMMUNITY

CNESS exists to unite conscious individuals, professionals, organizations, and partners into a thriving ecosystem of collaboration, knowledge sharing, ethical commerce, and personal and collective growth.

Our communities serve as spaces for:

  • Conscious conversations

  • Thoughtful knowledge exchange

  • Networking and collaboration

  • Leadership development

  • Marketplace and project initiatives aligned with conscious values

2. EXPECTATIONS OF BEHAVIOR

We expect all members of the CNESS community to uphold the following principles:

a.Respect and Courtesy:

  • Treat every individual with dignity and respect, regardless of background, profession, beliefs, or views.

  • Engage in dialogue with kindness, empathy, and active listening

b.Relevance and Constructiveness:

  • Contribute insights, questions, or content that are purposeful and relevant to the Platform's mission.

  • Offer constructive feedback; avoid personal attacks, hostility, or inflammatory language.

c.Inclusivity and Openness:

  • Foster an inclusive environment free from discrimination, harassment, or exclusion
  • Support diversity of thought and experiences.

d.Responsibility and Integrity:

  • Share accurate information to the best of your knowledge.

  • Respect confidentiality, intellectual property rights, and privacy expectations.

  • Represent CNESS and your affiliations honestly and ethically.

3. PROHIBITED CONDUCT

The following activities are strictly prohibited on the CNESS Platform and in all community interactions:

a.Harassment, Bullying, or Hate Speech:

  • Any form of harassment, threats, abuse, or intimidation.

  • Hate speech based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or any protected characteristic.

b. Unlawful Content or Conduct:

  • Promotion or engagement in illegal activities.
  • Sharing of illicit, obscene, sexually explicit, or violent materials.

c. Spam, Scams, and Self-Promotion:

  • Posting repetitive, unsolicited promotions or irrelevant commercial content.

  • Fraudulent schemes, phishing attempts, or misleading solicitations.

d.Privacy Violations:

  • Sharing others' private, confidential, or personally identifiable information without explicit consent.

  • Doxxing, recording, or distributing conversations without permission.

e.Intellectual Property Infringement:

  • Sharing content that you do not own or lack permission to distribute.

  • Misusing copyrighted materials, trademarks, or proprietary information.

4. COMMUNITY MODERATION AND ENFORCEMENT

CNESS reserves the right to monitor, moderate, and remove any content or account that violates these Guidelines or disrupts the Platform's integrity.

Enforcement Actions may include:

  • Content editing or removal

  • Issuing warnings

  • Temporary suspension from certain Platform features

  • Permanent termination of User accounts

  • Revocation of certifications, mentorships, partnerships, or directory listings in severe cases

Reporting Mechanism: If you encounter behavior violating these Guidelines, please report it immediately to support@cness.co. Reports will be reviewed confidentially and acted upon appropriately.

5. USER RESPONSIBILITIES

a.Self-Moderation:

  • Before posting, consider whether your contribution adds value and aligns with the spirit of consciousness and collaboration.

b.Respectful Disagreements:

  • Disagreement is welcome when expressed constructively. Attack ideas, not individuals.

c.Legal Compliance:

  • You are responsible for ensuring that your participation complies with applicable laws and regulations.

6. SPECIAL CONDUCT FOR MENTORS, PARTNERS, CERTIFIERS, AND MARKETPLACE PARTICIPANTS

Participants holding Mentor, Partner, Certifier, or Seller statuses must uphold elevated standards of professional and ethical conduct, including:

  • Acting in the best interest of CNESS Users and the broader conscious community

  • Providing truthful, high-quality services and information

  • Disclosing conflicts of interest

  • Upholding confidentiality agreements and client data privacy

Failure to adhere may result in additional disciplinary action, including removal from official CNESS Programs.

7. UPDATES TO THESE GUIDELINES

We may update these Guidelines periodically to adapt to evolving community needs, legal requirements, or Platform enhancements.

Material updates will be communicated through the Platform or via email. Continued participation after updates constitutes acceptance.
